In [Subspace Radio #89](https://www.subspace.fm/episodes/episode-89-theatre-sa-1x08-the-life-of-the-stars), Rob and I decide to stage this week's episode of the podcast, discussing "The Life of the Stars" (SA) as a piece of amateur theatre in a spare conference room on board the ship. We then reflect on other adventures upon the stage from episodes past: "The Conscience of the King" (TOS) and "Frame of Mind" (TNG).
